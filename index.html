Field Manual
  • WASD move, mouse aim, left mouse fire.
  • F opens doors, picks loot, enters vehicles, or opens boxes.
  • 1-6 select equipment; slot 5 throws grenades and slot 6 uses medicine.
  • Tab opens inventory. Right-click acts quickly; drag valid bag items.
  • M opens the tactical map with both current and next safe-zone rings.
  • Vehicles use W/S throttle and A/D steering; F exits.
About This Build

A rough original 2.5D survival-shooter prototype focused on map legality, loot, equipment, competing bots, vehicles, safe zones, and reliable match flow before multiplayer.
