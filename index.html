Field manual

How to Survive the Town

Everything needed for a first match, without leaving the game.

01

Loot and prepare

Search buildings and rich-loot districts. Use Tab to compare nearby ground loot, an opened container, your backpack, and equipped gear at the same time.

02

Fight deliberately

Aim with the mouse and fire with the left button. Weapons use specific ammunition. Melee attacks damage survivors, doors, and vehicles.

03

Read the circle

Open the tactical map with M. Move inside the white target circle before the blue zone closes; top-screen alerts announce each phase.

04

Use the town

Buildings provide hard cover and interior lanes. F opens intact doors and enters vehicles; destroyed doors remain as broken debris.

05

Drive and remember

Use W/S for throttle and A/D to steer. Your last intact vehicle is automatically marked on both maps.

06

Win the match

There are 13 survivors. Eliminate threats, rotate early, and become the final living participant. The result screen records placement, eliminations, and survival time.
